About Space

The Universe Awaits

☾

The Observable Universe

The observable universe is a spherical region of the universe consisting of all matter that can be observed from Earth. It has a diameter of approximately 93 billion light-years.

☼

Stars & Galaxies

There are an estimated 100-400 billion stars in our Milky Way galaxy alone, and over 2 trillion galaxies in the observable universe.

⍜

Dark Matter

About 27% of the universe is dark matter. It does not emit, absorb, or reflect light, making it extremely hard to detect directly.

Human Achievement

Space Exploration

1957

Sputnik 1

The Soviet Union launches the first artificial satellite, marking the start of the Space Age.

1961

First Human in Space

Yuri Gagarin becomes the first human to journey into outer space aboard Vostok 1.

1969

Moon Landing

Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin become the first humans to walk on the Moon during Apollo 11.

1990

Hubble Space Telescope

NASA launches the Hubble, revolutionizing our view of the universe.

2012

Curiosity on Mars

NASA's Curiosity rover lands on Mars to study the planet's climate and geology.

2021

James Webb Telescope

The most powerful space telescope ever built launches to peer into the early universe.
